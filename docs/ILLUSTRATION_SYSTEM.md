# 🎨 Vecina Digital Illustration System

> Every illustration should feel like it belongs to the same little neighbourhood.

Not because they share colours.

Because they share a soul.

---

# Purpose

Illustrations are not decoration.

They create emotional safety.

Someone visiting Vecina Digital should feel:

"I'm welcome here."

Before reading a single word.

# Artistic Direction

Every illustration should look hand painted.

Never AI-looking.

Never perfectly clean.

Never hyper realistic.

Every illustration should contain tiny imperfections.

Brush texture is welcome.

Paper texture is welcome.

Edges should feel organic.

---

# Line Work

Medium thickness.

Soft brown outlines.

Never pure black.

Never vector-perfect.

Lines should slightly vary in width.

Corners should be soft.

---

# Light

Always from the upper left.

Golden afternoon light.

Soft shadows.

Never dramatic lighting.

Never strong contrast.

---

# Colour Palette

Warm ivory

Soft cream

Terracotta

Warm wood

Olive green

Moss green

Warm grey

Golden yellow

Muted orange

Never saturated colours.

Never neon.

Never pure white.

Never pure black.

---

# Materials

Wood always feels natural.

Stone feels warm.

Plants feel alive.

Clay pots.

Natural fibres.

Nothing plastic.

Nothing metallic unless necessary.

---

# Shapes

Rounded.

Friendly.

Welcoming.

Nothing sharp.

Nothing aggressive.

Doors are rounded.

Windows have soft corners.

Roofs have gentle curves.

Plants grow naturally.

---

# Perspective

Slightly frontal.

Almost like a children's book.

Not isometric.

Not realistic perspective.

Everything feels approachable.

---

# Textures

Visible paper grain.

Watercolour washes.

Soft dry brush.

Gentle colour variation.

Never perfectly flat fills.

---

# Characters

Characters always smile with their eyes.

No exaggerated expressions.

Small blush is welcome.

Tiny imperfections make them lovable.

Technology is never cold.

Technology has feelings.

---

# Houses

Houses feel lived in.

Never luxurious.

Never perfect.

Always welcoming.

Always with personality.

---

# Doors

The door is the symbol of Vecina Digital.

Every door should communicate:

"You can come in."

Rounded arch.

Natural wood.

Heart detail.

Warm light.

Simple handle.

Stone surroundings.

Plants nearby whenever possible.

---

# Plants

Healthy.

Organic.

Rounded leaves.

Earthy colours.

Never tropical unless intentional.

---

# Composition

Lots of breathing room.

Illustrations should never feel busy.

One focal point.

Everything else supports it.

---

# Details

Tiny surprises are encouraged.

A little heart.

A leaf.

A flower.

A bird.

A cloud.

Never too many.

---

# Technology

Computers should feel friendly.

Not futuristic.

Not cyberpunk.

Technology exists to help people.

Never to impress them.

---

# What we avoid

Corporate illustration.

Flat illustration.

Gradient blobs.

3D renders.

Plastic.

Glassmorphism.

Sci-fi.

Tech startup aesthetics.

Neon.

Overly cute kawaii.

Emoji style.

Vector clipart.

Anything that feels generated.

---

# The Test

Every new illustration should answer YES to these questions:

Would this illustration belong in the same storybook as the Door?

Would a child want to touch it?

Would a local bakery owner feel welcome?

Would it still look beautiful printed on paper?

Would someone immediately think:

"That's Vecina Digital."

If not...

Redraw it.

# The Door Principle

Every illustration should answer one question:

"If this object had a little door...

...would I want to knock on it?"

If the answer is yes...

The illustration belongs in Vecina Digital.
